About

This calculator supports numeric suffixes: K, M, B, T, Qa/Qd (10^15), Qi/Qn (10^18), Sx (10^21), Sp (10^24), Oc (10^27), No (10^30), etc. Time units: D, H, M, S (case-insensitive).
